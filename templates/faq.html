Frequently Asked Questions

What is AEFI?

Adverse event following immunization (AEFI): A medical incident that takes place after an immunization, causes concern, and is believed to be caused by immunization AEFI surveillance: monitoring, detecting and responding to adverse events following immunization (AEFI); Implementing appropriate and immediate action to correct any unsafe practices detected through the AEFI surveillance system, in order to lessen the negative impact on the health of individuals and the reputation of the immunization programme.

What is ANM?

Auxiliary nurse midwife, commonly known as ANM, is a village-level female health worker in India who is known as the first contact person between the community and the health services. ANMs are regarded as the grass-roots workers in the health organisation pyramid. Their services are considered important to provide safe and effective care to village communities.

How is ANM beneficial?

Along with you, the ANM will also take care of the vaccination schedule of your child. In addition to that, if in any circumstances you have to file an AEFI, she'll automaically be notified that your child is in trouble

What is 'Set Reminder'?

You can add number of days before you want to get notified for the upcmoing vaccination schedule. You can also set frequency of reminders which will be responcible that how often you want to get notified before vaccination.

What if I don't set a reminder on 'Set Reminder'?

In such a case you will receive a notification a day before the immunization date by default.

What to do after immunization?

After immunization reach out to Vac. Status where you will have to update that which vaccination is provided to your child.
