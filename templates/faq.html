Frequently Asked Questions

What is AEFI?

Adverse event following immunization (AEFI): A medical incident that takes place after an immunization, causes concern, and is believed to be caused by immunization AEFI surveillance: monitoring, detecting and responding to adverse events following immunization (AEFI); Implementing appropriate and immediate action to correct any unsafe practices detected through the AEFI surveillance system, in order to lessen the negative impact on the health of individuals and the reputation of the immunization programme.

What is 'Set Reminder'?

You can add number of days before you want to get notified for the upcmoing vaccination schedule. You can also set frequency of reminders which will be responcible that how often you want to get notified before vaccination.
